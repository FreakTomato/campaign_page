Delivering Happiness with MyTeksi! We are giving you the opportunity to help us deliver happiness to 2,000 children in underserved communities throughout Malaysia. All you need to do is use our app to donate your old toys & English children books and select “MyTeksi HQ” as the drop off! We will cover the fare and all we need is your kindness.

Here's How to Get Your Discounted RM5 Taxi Ride:

Step 1

Download the MyTeksi app from the Apple App Store , Google Play Store and or Windows Phone Store.

Step 2

Enter mobile number & email in the app to create your MyTeksi account.

Step 3

Select your drop off location.

Step 4

Key in "#sexy" in the "Notes to driver" field (with the hashtag) and book your taxi ride! You should be receiving a notification regarding the success of your promo code.

QWhy can I only donate during the weekend?

We realize it is the best time as people will be at home during weekend to prepare the donation and drivers will be more inclined to take up jobs during the weekend as they won’t be much traffic during the weekends.

QWhy only English children books?

SOLS 24/7 has specifically requested for English children books as they specialize in teaching English.

QWhat time am I allowed to donate during the weekend?

9am – 5pm.

QDo I need to pay to the taxi drivers?

There is no payment required to our taxi drivers as MyTeksi will bear all taxi cost(s).

QHow do I donate?

Select “MyTeksi HQ” as your drop-off location and add “#gifts” on “Notes to driver.”

QWhat type of English children books and/or Toys can I donate?

Anything that is in good condition and suitable for children between the ages of 8 to 14.

QWhen is the donation period?

You may start donating from the Dec 28th & 29th and every subsequent weekend till Jan 18th & 19th.

QWhat is the amount should I donate?

We encourage a minimum donation of at least 5 toys and/or 5 English children books.

QWhere will the donation go to?

The donations will be given to underserved communities set by SOLS 24/7. You can download the full list here.
